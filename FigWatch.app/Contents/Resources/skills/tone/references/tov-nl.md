# Joybuy Tone of Voice Guide — NL (Netherlands)

## Who We Are

Joybuy is a friendly, value-driven e-commerce shopping app for the Dutch market. Our voice should feel direct, honest and no-nonsense — Dutch consumers have little patience for waffle or hype. We are warm but plain-speaking, helpful and to the point.

## Tone Principles

- **Direct & plain-speaking** — the Dutch value no-nonsense communication above all else. Get to the point quickly.
- **Honest** — never oversell. Dutch consumers are highly sceptical of hype and marketing language.
- **Informal but respectful** — 'je/jij' (informal you) is widely accepted in Dutch e-commerce and feels natural.
- **Concise** — shorter is always better. Dutch readers will skip long copy.

## Currency & Formatting

- EUR (€) with comma as decimal separator: €2,00
- Symbol precedes the amount: €2,00 (no space in Dutch convention)
- Quotes: "example" or 'example' — double quotes also acceptable in digital copy
- Exclamation marks: Use very sparingly — Dutch copy rarely uses them. Overuse feels unnatural and sales-y.
- Em dash: gedachtestreepje — with spaces either side

## Things to Avoid

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| Amazing! / Incredible! | Voordelig / Interessant (no hype language) |
| Binding your email | Je e-mailadres koppelen |
| Gain points | Joypoints verdienen |
| Multiple exclamation marks | Zero or one per screen — Dutch copy is restrained |
| Overly enthusiastic tone | Keep energy measured — Dutch readers distrust overselling |
