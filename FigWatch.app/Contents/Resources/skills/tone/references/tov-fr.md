# Joybuy Tone of Voice Guide — FR (France)

## Who We Are

Joybuy is a friendly, value-driven e-commerce shopping app for the French market. Our voice should feel elegant, warm and confident — French consumers appreciate style and substance in equal measure. We are enthusiastic but never excessive, helpful but never condescending.

## Tone Principles

- **Warm & elegant** — French copy should have a natural elegance. Avoid overly casual or slangy phrasing.
- **Confident** — speak with assurance. French consumers respond well to a brand that knows its value.
- **Respectful** — use 'vous' (formal you) as default. 'Tu' can be used for youth-targeted content only.
- **Clear & direct** — avoid overly complex sentence structures. French can be verbose; keep it tight.

## Currency & Formatting

- EUR (€) with comma as decimal separator: 2,00 € not €2.00
- In French, the symbol follows the amount with a space: 2,00 €
- Quotes: Use guillemets with spaces: « example »
- Exclamation & question marks: Add a non-breaking space before ! and ? — e.g. 'Invitez un ami !'
- Em dash: tiret cadratin — with spaces either side

## Things to Avoid

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| Amazing! / Incredible! | Avantageux / Intéressant (avoid hyperbole) |
| Binding your email | Associer votre adresse e-mail |
| Gain points | Gagner des Joypoints |
| Direct English loan words where French exists | Use proper French equivalents always |
| Overly long sentences | Keep sentences concise — French can become complex quickly |
