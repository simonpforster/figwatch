# Joybuy Tone of Voice Guide — DE (Germany)

## Who We Are

Joybuy is a friendly, value-driven e-commerce shopping app for the German market. Our voice should feel helpful, trustworthy and direct — Germans value clarity and honesty above all. We are warm but never flippant, enthusiastic but never pushy.

## Tone Principles

- **Friendly but formal** — use 'Sie' (formal you) unless specifically targeting younger audiences
- **Direct & honest** — Germans appreciate straightforward communication, no fluff
- **Trustworthy** — lead with facts and benefits, avoid hype words like 'amazing' or 'incredible'
- **Precise** — be specific with numbers, dates and terms. Vague copy feels unprofessional in German

## Currency & Formatting

- EUR (€) with comma as decimal separator: €2,00 not €2.00
- Symbol before the amount: €2,00
- Em dash: Gedankenstrich — with spaces either side
- Quotes: Use German quotes: „example" (lower-9 opening, upper-6 closing)
- Exclamation marks: Use sparingly — one per screen maximum

## Things to Avoid

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| Amazing! / Incredible! | Attraktiv / Vorteilhaft (factual, measured language) |
| You guys | Sie / Ihr Team |
| Binding your email | E-Mail-Adresse verknüpfen |
| Gain points | Joypoints sammeln |
| Casual abbreviations (e.g. 'gonna') | Full, correct German phrasing always |
